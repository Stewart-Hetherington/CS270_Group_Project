It's OK To Not Feel OK

Mental health is a important part of your overall health and happiness. Looking after your mental health and being mindful of your emotions leads you into a higher quality of life. Mental health issues are nothing to be ashamed of. Everyone in the world has or had some form of mental health related issue in their life at one stage or another. There is no courage without fear. Mental health issues such as depression, claustrophobia, anxiety and can be and are long term issues that require altering your daily lifestyle to overcome. However if you get a sudden or intensified onset of any of the conditions shown on screen, the tips in the boxes can help you get yourself into a better place and on a path to getting better forever. It is advised to seek help from a therapist to get you the peace of mind you deserve!

There are various websites and organisations out there that can offer even more support, help, and advice in Ireland. Some of the more notable ones are listed below:

  • Samaritans are an organisation that offer a phone service to get in contact with someone when they need to vent or simply to talk to someone.

  • Pieta House offer support & help to anyone who needs it, and are vital in the role of assisting people in dealing with self-harm and suicide.

  • Spunout is a site for youth to get support for their mental health, and more, and is run by youth as well. They provide articles on various topics that readers can sign up for and may be of great benefit to young people especially.

  • YourMentalHealth is a site to learn more about mental health, how to deal with and get support for mental health, and to help others in the same boat.

  • MentalHealthIreland is a voluntary organisation which aims to promote positive mental wellbeing in communities around Ireland. Their website contains numerous resources to aid on the path to dealing with mental health. They also organise fundraising & projects to assist those in need.

  • Aware specialises in dealing with depression. It offers support groups and information to help both you and friends & family around you.

  • Reachout is an online youth mental health service to help youth through tough times. The site offers mental health information, real-life experiences, advice, blogs, apps, and more.
